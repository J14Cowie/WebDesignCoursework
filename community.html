Community Outreach

As a group that is as dedicated to our community as we are to our work, we make a great effort to work local groups and charities in aims to create a community that is both thriving and safe
We aim to donate atleast 5% of our profits to charitable organisations around aberdeen, aswell as fund various events and endevours that further allow us to give back to the community

Plans for 2022

  • Hold events directed towards supporting local families in need
  • Expand the variety of skills and qualifications of our team to produce even higher quality web designs
  • Expand our portfolio to reflect the greater experience and increased skill we as a team have developed
  • Work with smaller local businesses, providing them with an online presence
  • Provide members of the society with the means to create their own web designs
